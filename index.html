Build-An-Aphrodite

About The Game


Using four of the more well-maintained Aphrodite statues seen on display at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, this game allows you to apply customizations to create the Aphrodite you desire to see. Throughout antiquity, the goddess of love was depicted in a variety of ways. In terms of the first known Aphrodite statue, scholars can only rely on written accounts from the likes of Lucian and other poets to understand what she might have looked at.
